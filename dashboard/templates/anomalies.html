📘 Detection Methodology

How It Works

  1. Compute mean (μ) and std deviation (σ) of power series
  2. Calculate Z-score for each point: Z = (x − μ) / σ
  3. Flag points where |Z| > 3.0 as anomalies
  4. Classify by Z-score magnitude: Low / Medium / High / Critical

Anomaly Types

  • Spike: Sudden surge (EV charging, equipment fault)
  • Drop: Sudden fall (outage, sensor failure)
  • Sustained: Prolonged deviation from baseline
  • Periodic: Unusual pattern on specific day/hour
